JSS TRUE Global

TRUE Global Education - Enable, Enrich and Enhance the Talent

Corporate is a great initiative taken by JSS TRUEGlobal. Intellectual minds at JSS TRUEGlobal are quite cerebral and realized the need of such a program for the benefit of their students. Corporate Connect lists out the development needs of students across different disciplines, frames modules to promote self improvement, valuable work experience and development among students.


Corporations around the world are looking to connect with JSS TRUEGlobal. We use most unique methodologies to work with their innovative and value-minded companies, large and small globally. With a strong global network of over 100 companies, JSS TRUEGlobal’s Corporate Connect platform helps providing internship to students by introducing and placing them in companies as an intern to gain practical hands on knowledge & experience.

We at JSS TRUEGlobal believe that internships using the corporate connect program will

  • Create learning atmosphere that fosters individual intellectual development
  • Enhance exposure to corporate through hands on experience and skill development
  • Promote motivation for further improvement with work experience that build’s individual’s confdence
