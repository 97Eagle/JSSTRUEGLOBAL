The students will have options to study in the Iowa Wesleyan campus at Mt Pleasant Iowa during their 4 years of degree program. The below are the four options based on the eligibility of the student

The following four options are available to eligible students. The eligibility is established during the second quarter of the degree program.
  • 1+3 Option
    • Study one year at JSS TRUE Global study center of Iowa Wesleyan university in Mauritius and study three years at the Mt Pleasant at Iowa USA
  • 2+2 Option
    • Study two years at JSS TRUE Global study center of Iowa Wesleyan university in Mauritius and study two years at the Mt Pleasant at Iowa USA
  • 3+1 Option
    • Study three years at JSS TRUE Global study center of Iowa Wesleyan university in Mauritius and study one year at the Mt Pleasant at Iowa USA
  • 3.5+0.5 Option
    • Study three years and 6 months at JSS TRUE Global study center of Iowa Wesleyan university in Mauritius and study six months at the Mt Pleasant at Iowa USA
